Approach & process

I design for people who work in complexity.

Enterprise software, financial operations, healthcare systems, AI platforms — these are the design problems I'm drawn to. Not because they're fashionable, but because the stakes are real and the design bar is chronically low.

My background spans product design and mechanical engineering — which means I think in systems, constraints, and feedback loops, not just screens. I bring that engineering rigour to every interaction model I design.

Currently at TCS, I'm designing AI-driven experiences where I have to solve hard problems: how does a user trust a prediction? What does uncertainty look like? When should AI surface a suggestion vs. stay quiet? These are the questions I find compelling.
